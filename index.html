Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ADU development in Boston

How long does the permit process take with your plans?

Our pre-approved plans typically receive BPDA approval in 2-4 weeks, compared to 3-6 months for custom designs. The ISD building permit usually follows within 1-2 weeks after BPDA approval.

What lot sizes do your plans work for?

Our plans are designed for three lot categories as outlined in Boston's ADU guidelines: Small lots (under 4,000 sq.ft) with studio options, Medium lots (4,000-6,000 sq.ft) with 1-bedroom options, and Large lots (over 6,000 sq.ft) with up to 2-bedroom ADUs.

Do I need to own the property for 3 years before building an ADU?

Yes, Boston requires owner-occupancy for 3 years after ADU completion. However, you can start the design and permitting process immediately - the 3-year requirement only applies after construction is complete.

Can I rent my ADU on Airbnb?

No, Boston ADU regulations require minimum 30-day rental periods. Short-term rentals (under 30 days) are prohibited for ADUs. This is designed to provide long-term housing rather than hotel-style accommodations.

What's included in your plan packages?

All packages include complete architectural drawings, BPDA compliance documentation, ISD permit application materials, owner affidavit templates, and digital files. Higher packages add structural engineering, site modifications, and ongoing support.

Do you work with contractors?

Yes! We maintain relationships with trusted contractors throughout Massachusetts who are familiar with our plans. We can provide recommendations based on your location, budget, and timeline preferences.
