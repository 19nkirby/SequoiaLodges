About Sequoia Lodges

Sequoia Lodges LLC is a Boston-based company specializing in the design, development, and marketing of pre-approved accessory dwelling unit (ADU) architectural plans for residential use. We offer a range of off-the-shelf modular designs that are code-compliant, permit-ready, and tailored to meet the unique zoning and construction requirements across Massachusetts.

Our goal is to help homeowners maximize the utility and value of their properties with beautifully designed, easy-to-execute ADU plans—whether for guest houses, in-law suites, income-generating rentals, or flexible living spaces. Each plan is crafted to blend aesthetic appeal with practical functionality, allowing for quick permitting, simplified construction, and long-term livability.

Inspired by the strength and longevity of the Sequoia tree, we deliver architecturally resilient and sustainable solutions that align with modern residential needs. With a deep understanding of local building codes and a commitment to streamlined project execution, Sequoia Lodges is redefining how Massachusetts builds backyard housing—one modular plan at a time.
