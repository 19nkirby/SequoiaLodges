How these numbers are built

Ranges reflect typical 2026 Massachusetts costs per square foot by ADU type (detached new build, garage conversion, attached/basement), adjusted by region, plus typical design and engineering fees. Rental estimates reflect asking rents for comparable small units by region. Every lot is different — utility runs, foundation conditions, and septic capacity move real numbers materially, which is why the next step after an estimate is a plan-fit read on your actual property.
