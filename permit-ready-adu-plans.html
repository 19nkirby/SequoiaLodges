FAQ

Common questions.

Do you handle the permit filing itself?

We produce the complete, filing-ready plan set and compliance documentation. You (or your contractor) submit it to the city — and because the set is built to Boston's requirements, review typically goes smoothly.

What can slow down the two-week timeline?

The most common factor is survey availability — if your property doesn't have a recent plot plan or survey, that can add time. Unusual site conditions can too. We flag anything like this up front.

Does this work outside Boston proper?

Yes. We serve Cambridge, Newton, Brookline, and surrounding Greater Boston communities. Requirements vary by municipality; the site-specific tailoring accounts for your local rules.

Do you build the ADU too?

No — we're a design and engineering firm, not a construction company. You receive a complete, permit-ready plan set and your contractor builds from it. That separation keeps our pricing fixed and our recommendations honest.

How much does a plan set cost?

$3,500 for a studio, $4,000 for a 1-bedroom (with structural engineering), and $4,500 for the 2-bedroom set with full structural engineering — our most requested package.
