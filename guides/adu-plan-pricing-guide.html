03How to choose

Choose the studio if the ADU is primarily for you — an office, studio, or occasional guest space — or if your buildable area is tight. It's the lowest-cost path to a permitted second structure.

Choose the 1-bedroom if you want a genuinely livable rental or in-law unit on a moderate footprint. The included structural engineering means the set is buildable as drawn, not just approvable.

Choose the 2-bedroom if the goal is rental income, housing family, or long-term property value — it's our most requested package for a reason. Two bedrooms command meaningfully higher rent in the Boston market, and the full stamped structural set means your contractor bids from complete documents, which typically means fewer surprises and tighter bids.

04What happens after you pick

Day 1 — Plan selection. You choose the package; we confirm fit against your lot details and survey.

Week 1 — Site-specific tailoring. Our principal engineer adapts the set to your property — setbacks, grades, access, utility routing — coordinated with licensed Massachusetts architects and engineers.

Within 2 weeks — Filing-ready set. You receive the complete package with compliance documentation, ready to submit for permit. Site conditions and survey availability can affect the schedule.

A note on site-specific modifications. Standard tailoring to your lot is included in every price above. Larger custom changes — a different footprint, an added level, accessibility features — are scoped and quoted by inquiry before any work begins, so there are never surprise fees.
