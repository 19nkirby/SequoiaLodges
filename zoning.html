Boston ADU Requirements:

  • Maximum 900 sq.ft (detached) or 50% of primary dwelling (attached)
  • Owner-occupancy required for 3 years
  • No short-term rentals (minimum 30-day leases)
  • 1 parking space required if zoning requires parking
  • Must maintain 15% open space on lot
  • Detached ADUs require 6' rear/side setbacks

Boston ADU Zoning Guide

R1 Districts

Single-family only. ADUs allowed as internal conversions (no size limit) or detached (max 650 sq.ft). Must maintain principal residence character.

R2-R3 Districts

Two-family allowed. Detached ADUs up to 900 sq.ft permitted with 6' rear/side setbacks. Internal conversions in existing structures allowed.

R4 Districts

Multi-family zoning. Attached and detached ADUs allowed with parking requirements (1 space per ADU if normally required).

All Zones

Must maintain 15% open space. No short-term rentals. Owner occupancy required for 3 years.
