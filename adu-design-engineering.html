The Scope

What the engineering actually covers.

A buildable ADU needs more than a pretty floor plan. This is the design and engineering scope behind every Sequoia Lodges set.

01

Architectural design

Dimensioned floor plans, all four elevations, and building sections — a complete, scaled drawing set.

02

Structural engineering

Framing and foundation design, included with 1-bedroom plans and up — ready for stamping and review.

03

Full MEP design

Mechanical, electrical, and plumbing layouts, included with the 2-bedroom package — the difference between a drawing and a buildable set.

05

FAQ

Common questions.

Can you modify a plan beyond the standard tailoring?

Yes. Site-specific adjustments are included with every package. Larger custom modifications — different footprints, added levels, accessibility features — are scoped and quoted by inquiry before any work begins.

Who stamps the drawings?

Every set is coordinated with licensed Massachusetts architects and engineers, and stamping is arranged as part of preparing your filing-ready set where the city requires it.

My lot is tight or sloped — can you still make it work?

That's exactly what the site-specific engineering pass is for. Tell us about the lot in the form below and we'll give you an honest read on fit before you commit to anything.

Do I need my own architect or engineer too?

No. Architecture, structural, and (with the 2-bedroom set) MEP design are part of the package. Your contractor builds from our coordinated set.
