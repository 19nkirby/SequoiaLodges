FAQ

Common questions.

Which plan is right for my lot?

Tell us your property city, lot type, and goals in the form below and we'll recommend the best-fit package — usually within one business day. Most Boston-area lots that fit a 2-bedroom footprint choose the 2-bedroom set for rental flexibility.

Is the price really fixed?

Yes. $3,500, $4,000, or $4,500 depending on the package, including site-specific tailoring. Only larger custom modifications — different footprints, added levels, accessibility features — are quoted separately.

How fast can I file for permit?

Most clients receive a filing-ready set within two weeks of selecting a plan. Site conditions and survey availability can affect the schedule.

What areas do you serve?

Boston, Cambridge, Newton, Brookline, and surrounding Greater Boston communities across Massachusetts.
